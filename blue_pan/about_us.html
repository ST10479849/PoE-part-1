Who we are:

Blue pan was launched in 2019, September 27th.It started as a hobby and the community loved our work, that’s when the founder Tshepo Refiloe Raboroko decided to turn it into a business, and sell cakes. As time went on, she saw that she had a love for catering to her community so she ventured into all kinds of ways to cater she started making platters and decorating too. The business was launched so that members of the community who could not afford aesthetically pleasing/professionally made cakes could have cakes made for them cakes that were aesthetically pleasing, healthy (no added artificial) and affordable. Blue pan strives for affordable services.

Mission Statement:

The mission of Blue pan is to reach as many people as possible to build a legacy for the business. The mission for Blue pan is to bring people together through food creativity and care.We strive to serve high quality cakes, beautifully arranged platters, and elegant decor that turn ordinary moments into extraordinary memories Our goal is not only to provide affordable and healthy options but also to deliver a heartwarming and unforgettable expirence.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become a pillar of creativity and growth within the community by not only offering delicious and affordable cakes, platters, and décor, but also by empowering people through baking lessons that inspire skill, confidence, and entrepreneurship. We aim to expand Blue Pan into a sustainable business that creates meaningful employment opportunities, nurtures local talent, and contributes to the economic well-being of our community. Ultimately, our vision is to build a brand that leaves a legacy of love, joy, and excellence—transforming simple gatherings into cherished memories while maintaining profitability and long-term growth.
